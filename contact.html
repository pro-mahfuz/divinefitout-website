Working Process

What usually happens after you contact us.

This page should not only capture leads, it should also reassure customers about what comes next after the first enquiry.

01

Send Your Enquiry

Customers can call, email, use the site form or start on WhatsApp with the basic project details and preferred service.

02

Review the Scope

The enquiry is reviewed to understand the finish type, project size, location and whether one or several services are needed.

03

Arrange Site Visit or Quote

Depending on the job, the next step is either a site visit, a material discussion or a more detailed pricing conversation.

04

Confirm and Schedule

Once the scope is clear, the project can move into confirmation, scheduling and the installation phase.

What should a customer send before a quote?

Floor plans, area dimensions, photos, preferred material type, expected timeline and whether the existing surface needs removal or repair all help speed up quoting.

Can one quote cover multiple services?

Yes. The site is designed around a multi-service workflow, so customers can ask about wooden flooring, tiles, marble, natural stone and carpet fitting in one enquiry.
