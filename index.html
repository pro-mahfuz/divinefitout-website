Working Process

How our Dubai flooring installation process works.

Every project follows a clear sequence so the finished surface looks refined, performs well and stays aligned with the material chosen for the space.

01

Site Visit

Measure the space, inspect the surface, note moisture risks and confirm access, timing and material choices.

02

Preparation

Handle leveling, priming, underlay, crack repair, cleanup and layout planning before any finish material is installed.

03

Installation

Fit the selected flooring or stone using the correct adhesive, joint spacing, cutting method and finish protection.

04

Finishing

Complete polishing, sealing, skirting, trims, transitions, cleanup and handover with maintenance guidance.
